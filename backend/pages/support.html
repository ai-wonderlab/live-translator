FAQ

How do credits work?
Easy Live Translator includes a free trial, then you buy translation time packs and use them as needed. There is no subscription.

What languages are supported?
The app supports live speech recognition, translation, and spoken playback across the languages available through Apple speech services and the app’s configured language pairs.

How is my privacy handled?
Read the privacy policy for details about microphone use, speech processing, and translation requests.
