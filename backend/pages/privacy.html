Easy Live Translator Privacy Policy

Easy Live Translator is designed to translate live conversations with as little data collection as possible.

How the app uses data

Your microphone is used only while you hold the talk button.

Speech recognition is handled by Apple, using on-device processing when available and Apple cloud processing when required by the platform.

Text that needs translation is sent to our OpenAI proxy so the app can return the translated result.

No account is required to use Easy Live Translator.

What we do not do

We do not sell your data.

We do not require you to create an account.
